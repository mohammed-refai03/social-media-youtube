The Story

How it all started

Founded in 2026, Stackly began as a simple idea: what if video streaming wasn't just about watching, but about truly connecting?

A group of passionate engineers and designers came together to solve the biggest problems in the creator economy—poor monetization, terrible algorithms, and lack of community tools. Today, Stackly is the fastest-growing platform for modern video.

Our Mission

To empower creators worldwide by providing them with the most advanced, community-focused tools to share their passions and build sustainable businesses.

Our Vision

To become the global destination for interactive entertainment, where every viewer feels like they belong and every creator can thrive.
